Bahariya & Black Desert

Palm-shaded oasis and dark volcanic hills en route to the White Desert.

Overview

Bahariya is a launchpad for desert safaris, with palm groves, hot springs, and the nearby Black Desert cinder-cone hills.

Top Experiences

  • Soak in oasis hot springs
  • Drive over Black Desert hills and viewpoints
  • Combine with White Desert camping circuits
