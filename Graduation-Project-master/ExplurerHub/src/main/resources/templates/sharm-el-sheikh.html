Sharm El-Sheikh

Gateway to Ras Mohammed’s protected reefs with lively resorts and nightlife.

Overview

From beginner-friendly bays to world-class wall dives, Sharm suits every level. On land, promenades, cafes, and shopping await.

Top Experiences

  • Boat trips to Ras Mohammed and Tiran
  • Snorkeling at Shark’s Bay and Naama Bay
  • Evening walks in SOHO Square
