Montaza Gardens

Palm-lined gardens and sea views around former royal palaces.

Overview

Expansive lawns, bridges, and Mediterranean vistas make Montaza a favorite for picnics and leisurely walks.

Things to Do

  • Stroll the waterfront paths and gardens
  • Photo stops by the palaces
  • Enjoy seaside cafes
